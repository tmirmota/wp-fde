FAQ

What is Foodee Rewards?

When you order with Foodee, you can earn points that are redeemable for various gift cards. It is our way of saying thank you for using Foodee.

How does Foodee Rewards work?

When you sign up for Foodee and start ordering, you begin earning points. Once orders have been delivered, the points show up in your account. Once you have enough points, you can redeem them for a gift card of your choice.

Who is eligible for Foodee Rewards?

Any team with a Foodee Meal Plan account will earn points with each order over $100. Different types of accounts earn points differently, you can find more information on Foodee Meal Plans here.

How do I earn points?

Basic/Pro accounts earn 1 Foodee Point for every $1 spent. Enterprise/Business accounts will earn 2 Foodee Points for every $1 spent.

What are the points worth?

For example, 200 Foodee Points are equal to $1 on Amazon. Foodee Rewards points are redeemable in increments of 50.

What do I get with points?

You can redeem your points for Amazon gift cards. More rewards coming soon!

How do I redeem my points?

Go to your dashboard, select the amount you want to redeem, and choose which gift card you would like. You will then receive an email with the gift card code and a link to the website of the gift card you choose.

If you have any questions, your Account Manager will be happy to answer them.

Are there any restrictions?

A couple. Only orders of a minimum of $100 are eligible for points. Foodee Rewards points are redeemable in increments of 50.

What if I’m already signed up for Foodee?

Everyone who uses Foodee is automatically added to Foodee Rewards. No need to sign up for anything extra.
