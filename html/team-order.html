Easy Steps to Team Ordering

Watch later, read now. Here's the TLDR to our easiest, most efficient way to order.

1. Set the details

Choose the restaurant, budget per person and your own order cut-off time for your team. You're almost done.

2. Send out the link

A unique team order link will be generated for you to share with your team. Anyone with this link can order to your shared cart.

3. Let your team pick

Watch your shared cart grow! You're still in control so you can add or remove orders before your order cut-off time.
